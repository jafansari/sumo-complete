SUMO - Simulation of Urban MObility

An open source traffic simulation package

"Simulation of Urban MObility" (SUMO) is an open source, highly portable, microscopic road traffic simulation package designed to handle large road networks.

It is developed by employees of the Centre for Applied Informatics (ZAIK) and the Institute of Transport Research at the German Aerospace Centre.

You may find the application and some examples within the download-section.

We kindly ask you to participate in any way - ask questions, report bugs, tell your needs.

New to SUMO?

  • If You have problems installing it, You may find help at wiki/BuildInstructions
  • If You have problems using it, You may find a tutorial at wiki/Tutorial.
  • Further documentation for users can be found within our documentation section and within the Wiki user section.
  • Please use the mailing lists (see Contact) to exchange problems or ideas.
  • Please put your projects and publications into the appropriate Wiki-section (follow the links).

SUMO - Simulation of Urban MObility

An open source traffic simulation package

"Simulation of Urban MObility" (SUMO) is an open source, highly portable, microscopic road traffic simulation package designed to handle large road networks.

It is developed by employees of the Centre for Applied Informatics (ZAIK) and the Institute of Transport Research at the German Aerospace Centre.

You may find the application and some examples within the download-section.

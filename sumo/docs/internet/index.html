SUMO is an open source, highly portable, microscopic and continuous road traffic simulation package designed to handle large road networks. It is mainly developed by employees of the Institute of Transportation Systems at the German Aerospace Center. SUMO is open source, licensed under the GPL.

Save the Date! The SUMO2014 Conference will take place May, 15th and 16th 2014 in Berlin-Adlershof.

Please cite SUMO in your publications as following: Daniel Krajzewicz, Jakob Erdmann, Michael Behrisch, and Laura Bieker. Recent Development and Applications of SUMO - Simulation of Urban MObility. International Journal On Advances in Systems and Measurements, 5 (3&4):128-138, December 2012. [bibtex] [pdf] [journal page]
